# gabo io chatbot content

This directory is dedicated to **gabo io ONLY** for grounded chatbot retrieval. It stores curated CX and lead-generation content that helps the chatbot answer website visitor questions with consistent, compliant, and service-oriented language.

## Retrieval rules

- Use this content only for gabo io chatbot responses.
- Keep answers concise, privacy-aware, and tied to Gabriel Services website content.
- Prefer the entry `answer` for direct responses and append the `leadGenerationPrompt` only when the visitor shows buying intent, asks for help, or needs follow-up.
- Do not treat this directory as a public site navigation section.

- `gabo-io-content-index.json`: Spanish Careers, Customer Relations Operations, Logistics Operations, Service Overview, Privacy & GDPR, Cookies Consent, and Terms & Conditions retrieval entries for CX and lead generation.
