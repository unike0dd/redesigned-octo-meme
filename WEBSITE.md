# Gabo Services Website Overview

## Purpose

The Gabo Services website presents practical operational support for organizations that want dependable help with recurring business activities. It gives visitors a clear view of the available service areas, explains how Gabo Services works, and provides direct paths for starting a conversation or applying for a role.

## Who the website serves

The website is designed for:

- Organizations exploring flexible operational support.
- Teams that need assistance with daily coordination and follow-through.
- Prospective clients comparing service areas before making an inquiry.
- Job seekers interested in opportunities with Gabo Services.
- Visitors who want to review the website's terms, privacy notice, or cookie notice.

## Main service areas

Gabo Services organizes its offering into four service areas:

1. **Logistics Operations** — support for dispatch coordination, shipment tracking, documentation, follow-up, inventory visibility, billing activities, and delivery timelines.
2. **Administrative Back Office** — support for scheduling, records, reporting, vendor coordination, travel planning, and recurring administrative workflows.
3. **Customer Relations** — support for customer inquiries, ticket follow-up, resolution tracking, billing clarification, service opportunities, and customer satisfaction activities.
4. **IT Support** — Level I and Level II assistance for intake, troubleshooting, user support, access requests, incident investigation, escalation, and resolution ownership.

## Visitor journeys

### Explore the company

The home and About pages introduce Gabo Services, its operating approach, and the value of consistent execution. Visitors can move from this overview into the service area most relevant to their needs.

### Review services

The Services page provides a single overview of the four service areas. Each service has a dedicated page with a summary of common activities, outcomes, and ways Gabo Services can support a client's day-to-day work.

### Start a conversation

The Contact page lets prospective clients describe their needs and submit an inquiry. The form guides visitors through the information needed to begin a productive follow-up.

### Explore careers

The Careers page lets applicants review opportunities and submit their professional information, experience, and areas of interest.

### Use guided assistance

The guided website assistant helps visitors navigate the website, understand service options, and find an appropriate next step.

## Website experience

The website provides:

- Responsive layouts for phones, tablets, laptops, and larger displays.
- Light and dark visual themes.
- English and Spanish content selection.
- Clear navigation across company, service, contact, career, and legal pages.
- Installable website behavior and a useful offline fallback.
- Shared visual patterns that keep pages consistent and easy to scan.

## Content structure

| Area | What visitors find |
| --- | --- |
| Home | Brand introduction, service summary, and primary next steps |
| About | Company positioning and working approach |
| Services | Overview of all service areas |
| Service detail pages | Activities, outcomes, and service-specific context |
| Contact | Inquiry form for prospective clients |
| Careers | Application form for prospective team members |
| Legal | Terms, privacy information, and cookie information |
| Offline | A simple fallback with a path back to the website |

## Intended outcome

The website should help each visitor quickly answer three questions:

1. What does Gabo Services do?
2. Which service area best fits the visitor's needs?
3. What is the clearest next action?

Its content and navigation are structured to move visitors from understanding to action without unnecessary complexity.
