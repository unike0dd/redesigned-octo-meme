# Gabriel Services Website

Gabriel Services presents practical business support for organizations that need dependable help with daily operations, customer experience, administration, logistics, and technology.

## Website experience

The website provides a clear overview of the company, its service areas, career opportunities, and ways to get in touch. Visitors can move between pages using a consistent navigation experience designed for phones, tablets, and desktop screens.

Content is available in English and Spanish through the language selector. Light and dark appearance options support different viewing preferences.

## Main sections

### Home

Introduces Gabriel Services and highlights the primary areas of support available to businesses.

### About

Explains the company’s practical, collaborative approach to helping teams maintain organized and reliable daily operations.

### Services

Presents four service areas:

- **Logistics Operations** — coordination, dispatch support, shipment visibility, and operational follow-up.
- **Administrative Back Office** — documentation, scheduling, reporting, records, and recurring administrative tasks.
- **Customer Relations** — customer correspondence, inquiry management, issue follow-up, and service continuity.
- **IT Support** — technical assistance, request organization, troubleshooting, and user-focused support.

Each service area has a dedicated page with an overview of its focus, typical activities, and delivery approach.

### Careers

Introduces available areas of interest and provides an application form for people who would like to work with Gabriel Services.

### Contact

Provides a structured form for organizations and visitors to describe their needs and request a conversation.

### Legal information

Includes the website terms, cookie notice, and privacy information.

## Visitor features

- English and Spanish content
- Responsive page layouts
- Light and dark appearance options
- Service-specific information
- Careers and contact forms
- Offline information page
- Installable website experience on supported devices
