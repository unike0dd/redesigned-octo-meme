## Core answer

IT Support provides structured Level I and Level II support for help desk intake, ticket creation, troubleshooting, end-user support, account access, escalation coordination, incident investigation, workflow support, root-cause analysis, and resolution ownership.

## Lead-generation guidance

When a visitor asks for IT support, collect contact details, ticket volume, affected user groups, systems involved, support hours, and escalation needs.

Suggested CTA: visit IT Support or the Contact page to request coverage.
