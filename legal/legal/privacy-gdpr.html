Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

1. Introduction

Gabriel Services ("we," "us," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information such as name, email address, and phone number
  • Service details and project requirements
  • Messages, inquiries, and communication records

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser information
  • Usage data, page visits, and navigation patterns
  • Cookies and local storage data for preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • Deliver and manage the services you request
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our website and service delivery
  • Comply with legal obligations and security requirements

4. Legal Basis for Processing

When you are in the EU or EEA, we process personal data based on GDPR legal grounds such as contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and consent.

5. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and protect our business interests.

6. Data Security

We maintain reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including encryption for data in transit and access controls for systems that process personal information.

7. Your Rights

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing activities.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or GDPR requests, please contact us through the Contact page on our website.
